# To Kill a Mockingbird Resources

## Knowledge

- [Book: *To Kill a Mockingbird* — Harper Lee (mass market paperback)](https://www.harpercollins.com/products/to-kill-a-mockingbird-harper-lee)
  The original novel. Use for: primary text for all lessons, quote citations, chapter analysis.

- [Audiobook: *To Kill a Mockingbird* narrated by Sissy Spacek](https://www.audible.com/pd/To-Kill-a-Mockingbird-Audiobook/B00TMDMWJE)
  Award-winning narration. Use for: listening comprehension, pronunciation of Southern dialect, immersive experience.

- [Film: *To Kill a Mockingbird* (1962) starring Gregory Peck](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0056592/)
  Classic adaptation. Use for: visual comparison, character portrayal analysis, courtroom scene study.

- [Article: "The Scottsboro Boys Trial: A Primary Source Account"](https://www.archives.gov/education/lessons/scottsboro)
  National Archives educational resource. Use for: Phase 5 historical context, connecting fiction to reality.

- [SparkNotes: *To Kill a Mockingbird*](https://www.sparknotes.com/lit/mocking/)
  Chapter summaries, themes, character analysis. Use for: review, essay preparation, quiz verification.

- [CliffsNotes: *To Kill a Mockingbird*](https://www.cliffsnotes.com/literature/t/to-kill-a-mockingbird)
  Detailed chapter-by-chapter analysis. Use for: deeper understanding of literary devices and themes.

- [GradeSaver: *To Kill a Mockingbird*](https://www.gradesaver.com/to-kill-a-mockingbird)
  Essay questions, character guides, study materials. Use for: essay writing preparation, peer review rubrics.

- [Shmoop: *To Kill a Mockingbird*](https://www.shmoop.com/to-kill-a-mockingbird)
  Modern analysis with humor. Use for: relatable explanations, vocabulary help, theme connections.

- [Innocence Project](https://innocenceproject.org/)
  Organization fighting wrongful convictions. Use for: modern connections to Tom Robinson's case.

- [Philippine Innocence Project](https://www.facebook.com/innocenceprojectphilippines/)
  Local context for presumption of innocence. Use for: PH relevance discussions.

## Wisdom (Communities)

- [r/Mockingbird](https://reddit.com/r/Mockingbird)
  Reddit community for discussing the novel. Use for: discussion questions, different perspectives.

- [r/Literature](https://reddit.com/r/Literature)
  Broad literary discussion subreddit. Use for: thematic analysis, comparative discussions.

- [Goodreads: *To Kill a Mockingbird*](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/2657.To_Kill_a_Mockingbird)
  Reader reviews and discussion. Use for: diverse interpretations, reading motivation.

- Local: School literature club or book discussion group
  Use for: real-time discussion, peer learning, wisdom sharing.
