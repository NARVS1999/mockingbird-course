Mga Simbolo at Motifs — To Kill a Mockingbird

The Mockingbird

Central Symbol Ang mockingbird ay isang ibon na kumakanta ng maganda pero hindi gumagawa ng masama. Ito ang pinaka-mahalagang simbolo sa buong nobela.
MockingbirdEbidensyaChapter
Tom RobinsonWrongfully accused, killed despite innocence — "senseless slaughter of songbirds"Ch. 25
Boo RadleyMistreated by father, pinagtsitsismisan, pero nagligtas ng buhay — "it would be like shootin' a mockingbird"Ch. 30
ScoutInnocent child na natututo ng tungkol sa mundoWhole novel
JemNasira ang paniniwala sa justice — victim ng broken systemCh. 21-22

The Mad Dog (Tim Johnson)

Symbol of Racism Ang rabid dog ay simbolo ng racism na kumakalat sa bayan — kailangang harapin at patayin.
ElementMeaning
Rabid dogRacism na nagiging masama sa bayan
Tim JohnsonIsang tao na na-infect ng racism

Motifs (Recurring Patterns)

  • Gothic elements: Radley house, mysterious events, dark nights
  • Small-town life: Everyone knows everyone, gossip, social hierarchy
  • Childhood innocence: Scout's perspective, games, imagination
  • Racial tension: Slurs, segregation, trial
  • Moral lessons: Atticus teaching Scout and Jem
