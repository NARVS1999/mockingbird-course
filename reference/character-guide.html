Character Relationships

Ang mga relationship sa nobela ay nagpapakita ng social dynamics:

  • Scout ↔ Boo: Mula sa takot patungong empathy
  • Atticus ↔ Tom: Abogado at client — mutual respect
  • Atticus ↔ Bob Ewell: Moral vs. immoral
  • Scout ↔ Calpurnia: Cross-race relationship
  • Jem ↔ Mrs. Dubose: Lesson on courage
