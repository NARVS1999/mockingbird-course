# Mission: To Kill a Mockingbird — Complete Course

## Why
Matututuhan ng mga estudyante ang nobelang *To Kill a Mockingbird* ni Harper Lee nang malalim — hindi lang plot, kundi ang mga tema ng racial injustice, moral courage, empathy, at loss of innocence. Ang layunin ay maging mapanuri at empathetic na mambabasa at mamamayan na kaya ring i-apply ang mga aral ng nobela sa totoong buhay.

## Success looks like
- Masusundan ang buong narrative arc mula Chapter 1 hanggang 31 nang may malalim na comprehension
- Ma-analyze ang mga character motivations, conflicts, at transformations
- Makilala at ma-explain ang literary devices (symbolism, irony, point of view)
- Maugnay ang fictional events sa totoong kasaysayan (Jim Crow, Scottsboro Boys)
- Makabuo ng sariling kritikal na essay o creative project
- Maipaliwanag kung bakit relevant pa rin ang nobela sa kasalukuyang panahon

## Constraints
- Taglish format para natural ang talakayan
- Interactive activities per chapter para sa deep understanding
- Self-paced online course via HTML lessons

## Out of scope
- Advanced literary theory (postcolonial, feminist theory deep dives)
- Comparison with other Harper Lee novels (Go Set a Watchman)
- Creative writing workshops outside the novel's context
